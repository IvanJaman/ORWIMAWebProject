Rage (1977)

The Rage is a psychological thriller novel that focuses on a high school senior who's prone to violent outbursts named Charlie Decker. After one such instance leads to his expulsion, Charlie gets a gun and takes one of his classes hostage, shooting teachers along the way. In the years since its publication, multiple actual school shooters appeared to be inspired by Rage, leading Bachman himself to request the book be taken out of print, where it's remained since the late-1990s.
